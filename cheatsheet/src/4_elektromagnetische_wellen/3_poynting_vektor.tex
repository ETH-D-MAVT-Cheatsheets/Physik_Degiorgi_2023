\subsection*{4.3 Poynting-Vektor}
    Energiestromdichte und Poynting-Vektor:
    \mathbox{\overrightarrow{S} = \overrightarrow{E} \times \overrightarrow{H} = \overrightarrow{j_E}}

    Energiedichte:
    \mathbox{|\overrightarrow{j_E}| \approx \rho_E = \frac{1}{2} \varepsilon_0 E^2 + \frac{1}{2} \mu_0 H^2}
