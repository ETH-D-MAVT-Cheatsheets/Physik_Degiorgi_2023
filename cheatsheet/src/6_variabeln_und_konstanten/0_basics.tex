            R                                               &\quad \text{el. Widerstand}                    & \scriptstyle \Omega = \frac{V}{A} \\
            S                                               &\quad \text{Siemens}                           & \scriptstyle S = \frac{1}{\Omega} = \frac{A}{V} \\
            T                                               &\quad \text{Periodendauer /}                   & \scriptstyle s \\
